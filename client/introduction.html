Why VAXSYS ?

Keeps you safe

Keep yourself healthy by taking timely vaccines, our reminders will keep you in check. You can rely on us to be safe. We are here to help you stay safe.

Keeps your near and dear ones safe

By early identification and prevention of potential risk of infection in you and others, VAXSYS enables better safety for those around you, acting as a shield of protection for all your loved ones.

Keeps Canada at large safe

The app helps the Govt. of Canada identify hotspots, hence aiding in curbing the spread of the infection. The more number of people use it, the more effective the website will be keeping everyone healthy.

Know about Us

VAXSYS is a vaccination management system for the people of Canada, that provides information on different types of vaccines for multiple, common, and infectious diseases like Chickenpox, Influenza, Polio, and Covid-19.
